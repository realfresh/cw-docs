# How to Add SEO (Search Engine Optimization to Your Landing Page

### Steps:

1. Log in to your account[ https://admin.cloudwaitress.com/login](https://admin.cloudwaitress.com/login)
2. Go to **Landing Page**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/unknown (1).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. Click **Search Engine Optimization**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/unknown (2).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4. Enter the **Website Title** and **Website Meta Description**, then **Save**.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/unknown (3).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
