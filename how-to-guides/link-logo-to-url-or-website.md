# Link Logo to URL or Website

Steps:

1. Log in to your account [https://admin.cloudwaitress.com/login](https://admin.cloudwaitress.com/login)
2. Once you're logged in, click "**Manage**"

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-11-11 at 1.38.22 PM.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. Go to **Settings**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-11-13 at 3.36.55 PM.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4. Go to **Website > Top Nav Bar > Logo link**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-11-17 at 8.13.26 AM.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

5. Add the **URL/ Website**  to link the logo image when clicked. Make sure to include http:// or https://
6. **Save**
