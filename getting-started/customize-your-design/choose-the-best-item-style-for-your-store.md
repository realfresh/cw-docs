# Choose the Best Item Style for Your Store

Explore the three item styles available in CloudWaitress and learn how to update or change them. Item styles determine how your product images are positioned and displayed, helping you choose the layout that best matches your store’s design.

### Steps:

1. Log in to your account[ https://admin.cloudwaitress.com/login](https://admin.cloudwaitress.com/login)
2. Go to **Settings** > **Website** > **Item Style**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (8).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. From the dropdown menu, choose from the following styles:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (9).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* **List** – Displays the item image on the left side of the item.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (10).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* **Card** – Displays the item image at the top, ideal for a visual, card-style layout.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (11).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* **Rounded List** – Shows your items in a clean list format with rounded edges, offering a modern and minimal look.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (12).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
