# Upload Favicon for Online Ordering Site

### Steps:

1. Log in to your account[ https://admin.cloudwaitress.com/login](https://admin.cloudwaitress.com/login)
2. Go to **Settings** > **Website**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (20).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. Navigate to **Favicon** and click **Upload**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (21).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4. Upload your favicon image. We recommend a **350x350 pixel square**. Make sure the file name **does not contain special characters**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (22).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once uploaded, your favicon will appear on your browser tab and bookmarks, confirming it is active.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (23).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
